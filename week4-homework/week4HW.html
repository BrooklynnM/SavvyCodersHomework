Let's Roller Skate

Did you know there are many benefits to roller skating? Besides looking super fly, roller skating works both your body and your mind and has proven to be an effective workout with substantial health benefits. Not only is roller skating good from a physical fitness standpoint it is also great for your mental health too! Let's discus the recent popularity of roller skating and why you should give it a try!

According to the Roller Skating Association Inernational, skating works every muscle in your body and can burn anywhere from 350 to 600 calories per hour. Along with calorie burning, it is a great way to improve your heart health and strengthen muscles. It works every muscle in your body including the legs, glutes, arms, and core. Roller skating is 50% less strenuous to the joints than running is. It even improves flexibility and endurance, along with improving your balance and coordination.

Recap of Benefits

  • Full Body Work Out
  • Improved Heart Health
  • Muscle Strengthening
  • Flexibility & Endurance
  • Balance & Coordination

How to get started!

  1. Find yourself a pair of skates. Any skate will do!
  2. Find a flat low traffic area to practice.
  3. Places to skate:
    • Outdoor Parks
    • Indoor Skating Rink
    • Empty Parking Lots
    • Tennis Court
  4. Hit the road!
